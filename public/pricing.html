Frequently Asked Questions

How do you count requests? +
Each API call to your inference models counts as one request. Cached responses and batch operations are counted separately.
Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime? +
Yes, you can change plans at any time. We'll prorate your billing based on usage. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.
Do you offer annual discounts? +
Yes! Enterprise customers get 20% off annual plans. Contact our sales team for more details on volume pricing.
What if I exceed my plan limits? +
We'll notify you when approaching limits. You can upgrade immediately, or we can discuss custom overage rates for your use case.
Is there a setup fee? +
No setup fees. Start using InferenceIQ immediately after signing up. Your first month is prorated based on usage.
