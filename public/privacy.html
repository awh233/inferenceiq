Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 7, 2026

1. Introduction

InferenceIQ Inc. ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and otherwise process personal information in connection with our services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, subscribe to our service, or contact us for support. This includes:

  • Account information (name, email, company, password)
  • Payment information (billing address, payment method)
  • Communication data (support tickets, feedback)
  • API usage data and logs

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Process payments and send related information
  • Send promotional communications (with your consent)
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide support
  • Monitor and analyze service usage trends
  • Detect and prevent fraudulent activity
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy. You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at privacy@inferenceiq.com.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Restrict our processing of your information
  • Receive a copy of your information in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent at any time

7. Third-Party Services

We do not share your personal information with third parties except as necessary to provide our services, comply with legal obligations, or with your explicit consent. We use the following third-party services:

  • Payment processors (encrypted and PCI-compliant)
  • Email service providers (for communications)
  • Cloud infrastructure providers (for data storage)

8. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings. Essential cookies cannot be disabled as they are necessary for service operation.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected such information, we will promptly delete it.

10. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or to exercise your rights, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@inferenceiq.com
