Our Mission

Our Story

InferenceIQ was born from a simple observation: teams were spending thousands monthly on AI inference while optimizing inefficiently. We realized that with better routing, caching, and batch processing, we could cut costs by 40% without sacrificing quality.

Founded in 2025, we set out to build the infrastructure layer that every AI team needs—invisible, automatic, and incredibly effective. Today, we're helping companies optimize billions of tokens monthly.
