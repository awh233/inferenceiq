Regulatory Requirements

GDPR Compliance

InferenceIQ complies with all GDPR requirements including:

  • Lawful basis for processing (contractual necessity)
  • Data subject rights (access, rectification, erasure, portability)
  • Data Protection Impact Assessments (DPIA) available upon request
  • Breach notification within 72 hours
  • International data transfers via Standard Contractual Clauses

CCPA Compliance

We comply with California Consumer Privacy Act requirements:

  • Right to know: Access all personal information we've collected
  • Right to delete: Request deletion of personal information
  • Right to opt-out: Decline sale or sharing of information
  • Right to non-discrimination: No penalization for exercising rights
  • Verifiable consumer requests handled within 45 days

Data Protection

All customer data is protected under our comprehensive data protection program:

  • Encryption at rest and in transit
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular security assessments and penetration testing
  • Incident response procedures
  • Data retention and deletion policies

Certifications

Current certifications and ongoing compliance efforts:

  • SOC 2 Type II audit (in progress, Q2 2026)
  • ISO 27001 certification (planned Q3 2026)
  • Regular third-party security audits
