Our Mission

Our mission is to make AI inference accessible, affordable, and reliable for every team.
We believe that advanced AI inference shouldn't be limited to companies with massive budgets. Today, many teams waste significant resources on inefficient inference pipelines. InferenceIQ was founded to democratize access to the best inference routing technology, enabling startups and enterprises alike to optimize their AI operations.

Our Story

Founded in 2024

InferenceIQ was founded by a team of AI infrastructure experts who saw firsthand the challenges teams face when deploying inference at scale. We spent years working at leading AI companies and decided to build the solution we always wanted.

Based in San Francisco

We're headquartered in San Francisco, at the heart of the AI innovation ecosystem. Our location allows us to stay close to customers, top talent, and the latest research in large language models and inference optimization.

Backed by Top-Tier Investors

We're fortunate to be backed by leading venture capital firms and angel investors who share our vision. Their support enables us to invest in R&D and build the best product in the market.

Growing Fast

From day one, we've focused on building a product that solves real problems. Our customers report 40-70% reductions in inference costs and 99.9% uptime with our platform. We're scaling rapidly to meet demand.

Our Values
