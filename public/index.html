SUPPORT

Frequently Asked Questions

How does InferenceIQ maintain quality while reducing costs?

▼
InferenceIQ uses quality scoring and semantic similarity detection to ensure that cost optimizations never compromise output quality. You maintain full control over quality thresholds, and the system respects them automatically.

What's the integration process like?

▼
Integration typically takes 5-15 minutes. For most frameworks, you simply point your requests to our proxy endpoint instead of the provider's API. No code changes required in most cases. We provide SDKs for Python, Node.js, and Go.

Do you store or use our data?

▼
We never store your prompts or completions beyond the minimum needed for analytics and debugging. All data is encrypted in transit. Enterprise customers can deploy InferenceIQ in their own VPC with zero data leaving their infrastructure.

What about latency and reliability?

▼
InferenceIQ adds <10ms latency in 99.9% of cases due to our edge-optimized infrastructure. We maintain 99.99% uptime SLA with redundant routing, automatic failover, and distributed processing across multiple regions.

Can we use InferenceIQ with multiple AI providers?

▼
Yes! Multi-provider support is core to InferenceIQ. You can route to OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, Meta, Groq, DeepSeek, and any OpenAI-compatible API. The system automatically selects the best provider for each request.

What kind of savings can we expect?

▼
Average savings are 30-50% of AI inference spend depending on your workload and optimization strategy. Some customers see 60%+ savings with aggressive optimization. We provide detailed per-request attribution so you see exactly where savings come from.

Is there a minimum contract or long-term commitment?

▼
No lock-in contracts. Start with our free tier, upgrade as needed. Enterprise customers can discuss custom terms. You can cancel anytime with 30 days notice.
