Why teams choose InferenceIQ

Every feature designed to eliminate waste and maximize efficiency in AI inference workloads.

Intelligent Batching

Automatically batch requests to maximize GPU utilization and reduce per-token costs by 40%+. Context-aware scheduling ensures no performance degradation.

Smart Caching

Multi-tier caching strategy with semantic deduplication. Cache hits on similar prompts reduce latency to <1ms and eliminate redundant compute.

Dynamic Model Routing

Route requests to the most cost-effective model without sacrificing quality. Real-time latency and accuracy thresholds ensure SLA compliance.

Cost Attribution

Drill down to exact cost per feature, endpoint, user, or team. Chargeback models integrated with your billing system for complete visibility.

Real-time Alerting

Instant alerts on cost anomalies, p99 latency spikes, and quota breaches. Custom thresholds per model, endpoint, or time of day.

Automated Recommendations

Machine learning-powered suggestions for batch sizes, cache TTLs, and model selection. Actionable insights that require one-click deployment.
