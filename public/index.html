HOW IT WORKS

Three steps to lower costs

01

Connect Your Models

Point your API calls to InferenceIQ. We support all major LLM providers with a drop-in replacement.

base_url="https://api.inferenceiq.com"
02

Optimize Routing

Define your preferences: cost, latency, or quality. InferenceIQ automatically routes to the best provider.

optimize="cost" # or "latency" or "quality"
03

Monitor & Scale

Real-time analytics, cost breakdowns, and instant insights into your API usage and spending.

response.cost, response.provider, response.latency_ms
